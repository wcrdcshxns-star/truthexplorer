The Last Bench

In a government school in Bihar, a boy who sits at the last bench discovers that the view from the back reveals truths the front row never sees. A story about invisibility, dignity, and the quiet revolution of a child who teaches himself to read while the teacher faces the blackboard...

🌆 Urban Life · Mumbai

Signal No. 7

Every day at signal number 7 on the Western Express Highway, a girl from Dharavi sells flowers. One evening, she meets the son of the man whose company is building on her village's land. Neither of them will forget what was said in those thirty-five seconds before the light turned green...

🌾 Rural India · Rajasthan

The Well at the Edge of Town

A seven-year-old girl in a Rajasthani village walks two kilometers to fetch water every morning. The well at the edge of town is old, wise, and — she is certain — alive. A magical realist fable about scarcity, womanhood, and the stories water carries across generations...

LRLakshmi Rajput·Class 9 · Maharani School, Jaipur·10 min readRajasthani
